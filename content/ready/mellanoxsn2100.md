The Mellanox Technologies SN2100 is a Spectrum(TM) based 40GbE 1U Open Ethernet switch with MLNX-OS,
with 16 QSFP28 ports, 2 power supplies (AC), Rangeley CPU, short depth, P2C airflow, Rail Kit, RoHS6.
